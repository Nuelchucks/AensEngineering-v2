About AENS Project & Engineering

AENS is a multidisciplinary engineering firm providing end-to-end solutions across energy, industrial and infrastructure sectors. We combine local knowledge with international best practice to deliver safe, cost-effective projects on schedule.

Who We Are

Founded to deliver practical engineering solutions, AENS brings together experienced engineers, project managers and technicians to serve clients in power, oil and gas, manufacturing and infrastructure.

Our approach focuses on safety, quality and sustainability. We work at every stage of a project from Feasibility and Detailed Design to Procurement, Construction and Commissioning.

Mission

To be one of the leading Engineering Services and Solutions providers in Africa, by providing our valued clients with dedication, cooperation, innovation, competitive pricing, highest service, qualities and operational efficiencies in the Energy, Oil and Gas, manufacturing, and Construction Industries.

Vision

To provide exellent quality of services and products that deliver maximum value to our clients in Nigeria and around the world.

Core Capabilities

We offer comprehensive engineering services across multiple disciplines to ensure project success.

Project Management

Planning, scheduling, cost control and delivery assurance for projects of varied scale.

Engineering & Design

Multidisciplinary engineering across electrical, mechanical and civil disciplines.

Commissioning & Testing

Site testing, commissioning support and performance verification to ensure systems operate to spec.
